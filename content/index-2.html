Reserve Your Table

Make sure you get a seat during the busiest time of the year!

All tables offer a great view and listening experience of the lve music. If you reserve a table the waiting time is 5 minutes if you reserve, or a free dessert on the restaurant.

Before reserving we would love for you to explore our website and the dishes we offer at the restaurant. Even if you are at the restaurant and there is a waiting time, you can explore the bakery and its amazing selection of treats.

Customer Loyalty Program

At Earthly Creations we value our customers and want to provide them with somethign special everytime they attend!

Seeing that over 90% of our customers left during the 2008 crises, we wanted to reward the remaining amount for their loyalty.We offer a restaurant credit card for all members to use if desired. The more they come and eat, the more reward points will be stacked on their credit card.The reward points can be used to buy gift cards for “Earthly Creations” and other local restaurants around that are also supported by the local high school.
