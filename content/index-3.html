Our Story

Earthly Creations is a family owned business. Thought of in the early 20000s, mainly as a joke, our family, the Bearebs, have grown this business beyond what we would have thought. Starting in 2006, we finally made this joke into a reality. We made the name relate to our last name “bear,” seeing that bears live in the wilderness of the earth. Our 4th sister of a total of 5 sisters and 3 brothers, who was probably the best cook of us all back in the younger ages, laughed at our idea and denied our request for her to join the business. She thought that due to her gluten free diet she would feel awkward despite her many jobs at high end restaurants. Although we were distraught at her refusal, we continued with the idea and made “ Earthly Creations.” After our very successful start up and multiple requests, our 4th sister had finally agreed to join us, making us a full restaurant with every family member, including now a very popular baker around town. We then decided, to also celebrate her decision, to make the restaurant gluten and wheat free to server everyone like our sister. However, that happiness was very ephemeral. With the housing crises in 2008, we almost went bankrupt, losing just about 90% of our customers; we were scared. We thought that nothing could beat us down, but seeing that only one scare almost brought us down, we have now decided to make every decision together and in logical ways to reduce risks. Now, in 2016, we still have the entire family operating in this restaurant, working with each other almost everyday to serve every customer, including the wheat and gluten free population, the satisfaction they desire and deserve. Our goal right now is to expand our business beyond the Brunswick Area into Edison as well as Trenton.
